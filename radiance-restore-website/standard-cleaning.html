Standard Clean vs. Deep Clean: What's Actually Different?

The three things that take the most extra time and labor in a Deep Clean:

  • Cleaning inside all appliances except the refrigerator (fridge interior available as an add-on)
  • Blinds and shutters hand-washed and wiped, not just dusted
  • Baseboards hand-washed and scrubbed, not just dusted

A Deep Clean is also a much more thorough overall dusting of everything, including light fixtures and ceiling fans.
