Frequently Asked Questions

When should this happen?

After contractors finish and before move-in, ideally with furniture not yet placed.

Can you handle heavy dust and debris?

Yes, this is a specialized service built specifically for post-renovation conditions, including a full dust-quality inspection at the end.

Do you remove paint splatter or adhesive residue?

Light residue, yes, heavy paint or adhesive removal may require a specialist and can be discussed case by case.
